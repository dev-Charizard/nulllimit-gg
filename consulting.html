Common questions
Do I need a technical team to use AI?
No. Everything we build is designed to be managed by non-technical staff. The whole point is that your team can run it without needing a developer on call.
How long does implementation take?
Single solutions typically take 1–2 weeks. Full stack engagements run 4–8 weeks depending on complexity. The goal is always to get something working and generating value in the first 30 days.
What tools and platforms do you work with?
We work with OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, OpenClaw, Zapier, Make, and most major business platforms (Shopify, HubSpot, Notion, Slack, Gmail, and more). If your tool has an API, we can connect to it.
What if I don't know what I need?
Start with the Discovery Call. That's exactly what it's for — figuring out where AI can help your specific business before committing to anything bigger.
Is my data safe?
Yes. We only use enterprise-grade AI APIs with strict data handling policies. We never train AI models on your customer data and we follow data minimization principles by default.
What's the typical ROI?
Industry data puts average AI implementation ROI at 3.5x, with most businesses reaching positive ROI within 60–90 days when targeting high-volume repetitive tasks. We'll give you specific estimates for your business before you commit.
