01 — What we analyze

Six signals.
One edge.

Every report covers six data categories across all featured NBA markets. The last one is the one nobody else has.

01
Injury & Roster Impact

Real-time injury reports, minutes projections, and lineup confirmations. How much does losing player X actually change the team's ceiling?

Why it matters: Markets often misprice the true impact of a role player injury. We quantify it.

02
Travel Fatigue Score

Back-to-backs, 3-in-4 games, cross-timezone road trips, altitude changes. Teams don't perform the same on the second night of a back-to-back in Denver.

Why it matters: Publicly available data, chronically underpriced in NBA markets.

03
Pace & Matchup Analysis

Team pace ratings, tempo differential, defensive matchup vulnerabilities. Fast teams vs slow teams play completely different games.

Why it matters: Pace determines how many possessions — which determines how many points.

04
Home/Away Splits

True home court advantage by team, road record weighted by opponent strength, performance against the spread on the road vs at home.

Why it matters: Home court advantage varies massively by franchise. It's not a flat 3 points.

05
Market Price Analysis

Current yes/no prices on Kalshi and Polymarket for all featured games. Where the market is priced, where it's moved, and what the implied probability is.

Why it matters: You need to know what the market thinks before you decide what you think.
